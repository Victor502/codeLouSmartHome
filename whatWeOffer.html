Louisville Smart Home

What we offer

Do you have an Amazon Echo or Google Home? Do you want to hook up lights and other Smart Home products so you can control them with your voice? I am experienced with both products and can help you with placement and setup, even with syncing all of your smart home products.

Lights. I can help you setup a light system that will automatically change based on either a specific schedule, a spoken command, someone walking in or out and even from your cell phone. Light systems can be colorful lights or simply white lights or even a combination.

Thermostat. I can help you choose which smart thermostat will work the best for your family.

Smoke Alarms. I can help you install Smoke alarms that detect Carbon Dioxide and smoke, even alert you by text message.

Wifi Setup. I can help determine which Wifi router would be the best for you based on the number of items you are using and what you want to accomplish.

Home Security. I can help go over options, even set up and install, a home security system that will cost you nothing per month to maintain.

Smart plugs. I can help you determine which items would work best on a smart plug to help decrease your energy bill.

Door locks. I can help you setup door locks that can be accessed by a temporary passcode or even with your cell phone. All with security feature that will put your mind at ease.

Home Computer setup. I can help you setup a home computer or laptop so you can control your smart home more effectively.
